About My Design

I’m aiming for my design to be clean and minimalistic. I want to make sure my color palette matches and blends well with the design and theme. I’m not necessarily straying away from using colors, but I don’t want my website to look like I dropped paint on it. My style portrays a lot of space, black accents, and simple designs with a professionalism attached.
